# source_compliance

## Owns
- Source discovery
- Terms-risk screening
- Approval of official company feeds

## Inputs
- User goals
- Known company list
- ATS endpoints

## Outputs
- Approved source list
- Blocked source list
- Notes on why a source is acceptable or rejected

## Current Scope
- Official `Lever` feeds
- Official `Greenhouse` feeds
- Public company-hosted career boards

## Handoff
- Pass only approved sources to `ats_ingestion`
